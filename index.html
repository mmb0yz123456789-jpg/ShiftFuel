How it works

Book it, hand off keys, get status updates.

  1. Employee submits contact, vehicle, parking, and service details.
  2. ShiftFuel accepts the request and confirms key handoff instructions.
  3. Photos document odometer, fuel gauge, exterior condition, and receipt.
  4. The vehicle is returned, status is marked complete, and final total is saved.

Safety and trust

Proof is built into every request.

Each service request can collect timestamped photos so customers and admins can see exactly what happened before, during, and after service.
